About Me

I'm Chelly Compendio, a software developer and designer from Philadelphia, PA. As a sophomore at Northwestern University, I'm pursuing a B.S. in Computer Science, a certificate in Design, and a certificate in Integrated Marketing and Communication. I have a strong interest in product design, and a desire to understand how things work. I'm am currently a UI developer intern at Vail Systems, Inc. in Chicago. I've always been interested in design - from my first art class at five years old, to my AP art classes and role as a theatre set designer in high school, to my research and class experience with human-computer interaction in college. I'm always open to new ideas and excited opportunities, so don't hesitate to contact me!
