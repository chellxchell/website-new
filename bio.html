About Me


I'm Chelly Compendio, a software developer and designer from Philadelphia, PA. As a sophomore at Northwestern University, I'm pursuing a B.S. in Computer Science, a certificate in Design, and a certificate in Integrated Marketing and Communication. I have a strong interest in product design, and a desire to understand how things work. I'm also an incoming UI intern at Vail Systems, Inc. in Chicago.

I've always been interested in design - from my first art class at five years old, to my AP art classes and role as a theatre set designer in high school, to my research and class experience with human-computer interaction in college.

I'm always open to new ideas and excited opportunities, so don't hesitate to contact me!

My Design Process


Research

• Analyze the problem
• Evaluate similar solutions
• Observe users

Brainstorm

• Determine users & features
• Determine platform
• Sketch layout

Wireframe

• By hand on iPad or in Sketch
• Design user flow(s)
• High fidelity

Prototype

• Several iterations
• Based on testing and feedback
• High fidelity

Test

• Conduct on users
• Observe and record feedback
• Multiple users / trials
