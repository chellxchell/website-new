Mainline Behavioral Medicine



Mainline Behavioral Medicine is a project for a private psychiatry practice in Paoli, Pennsylvania. My goal was to make the site look professional, but still simple enough that users can find information easily. The project is a one page website, so that all of the information is easily accessible. I also included a navigation bar with the titles of each individual section. I worked out a color scheme that included a dark blue and a lighter blue for highlights.

I also designed the lettermark logo in the primary color I used, although it appears white on the website for visibility. I wanted the logo to be simple but recognizable.
